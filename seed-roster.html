Seed the chapter roster
Run this once after setting up Firebase
What this does: Uploads every row in your CSV/Excel file as a brother in the Firestore roster collection. After running it once, you never need to come back here — use the regular Roster tab in the app for ongoing edits.
  1. Sign in with your exec Google account (top right).
  2. Click the button below and pick your CSV or Excel file. seed-roster.csv in this repo is a ready-to-use file pre-populated with your current chapter roster.
  3. Confirm the count, then push to Firestore.

Next steps

Once the roster is in Firestore, head back to the main app and you're done. Brothers can check in immediately. Exec can add, remove, or import more brothers from the Roster tab.
